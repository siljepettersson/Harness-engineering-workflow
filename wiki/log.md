# Wiki Log

Append-only maintenance log for wiki operations.

Use entries such as:

```text
## [YYYY-MM-DD] ingest | Source title
## [YYYY-MM-DD] query | Question summary
## [YYYY-MM-DD] lint | Check summary
## [YYYY-MM-DD] promote | Answer promoted to wiki page
```

The log helps future agents understand what changed recently and why.
